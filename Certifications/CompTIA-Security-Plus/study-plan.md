## Daily Study Routine Template

### Morning Routine (Optional - 30 min before work)
- Review 20-30 flashcards
- Read 1-2 pages of study notes

### Evening Study Session (1.5-2 hours)
1. **Warm-up (15 min)** - Review previous day's notes
2. **New Content (60-90 min)** - Videos, reading, note-taking
3. **Active Practice (30 min)** - Labs, simulations, or hands-on
4. **Quiz (15 min)** - Practice questions on today's topics

### Saturday Intensive (4 hours)
1. **Deep Dive (2 hours)** - Complex topics or hands-on labs
2. **Break (30 min)** - Rest and recharge
3. **Practice Test (1.5 hours)** - Full section or practice questions
4. **Review (30 min)** - Analyze results, update notes

### Sunday Review (2 hours)
1. **Weekly Review (1 hour)** - Go through all week's notes
2. **Flashcard Creation (30 min)** - Make new flashcards
3. **Practice Questions (30 min)** - Quiz on week's topics

## Adjustment Protocol

If falling behind or struggling:

### Option 1: Extend Timeline
- Add 2-3 weeks for additional review
- Push exam date back
- Don't sacrifice quality for speed

### Option 2: Increase Study Time
- Add 30 min per day
- Utilize lunch breaks
- Wake up earlier for flashcard review

### Option 3: Focus Strategy
- Identify critical domains (4 and 2 are largest)
- Prioritize weak areas
- Accept "good enough" on strong areas

## Emergency Revision Plan

If exam is in 2 weeks and you haven't studied enough:

- 3-4 hours daily minimum
