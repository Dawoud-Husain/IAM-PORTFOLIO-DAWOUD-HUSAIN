### 5.2 Explain elements of the risk management process

#### Video 1: Risk Management
- [ ] **Watched Professor Messer's "Risk Management" video**

##### Risk Management Basics
- [ ] **Risk definition**
  - Likelihood of threat exploiting vulnerability
  - Impact on organization
  - Uncertainty
  - Measured and managed

- [ ] **Risk assessment types**
  - **Ad hoc assessments**
    - As-needed basis
    - Specific concerns
    - New projects
    - Change-driven
  - **Recurring assessments**
    - Regular schedule
    - Annual, quarterly
    - Continuous monitoring
    - Ongoing process

- [ ] **Risk assessment methodologies**
  - **Qualitative**
    - Subjective
    - Low, Medium, High
    - Quick and easy
    - Less precise
  - **Quantitative**
    - Objective
    - Numerical values
    - Cost-based
    - More precise but time-consuming

##### Risk Factors
- [ ] **Likelihood**
  - Probability of occurrence
  - Historical data
  - Threat intelligence
  - Environmental factors

- [ ] **Impact**
  - Potential damage
  - Financial loss
  - Reputational harm
  - Operational disruption
  - Legal consequences

- [ ] **Risk appetite**
  - Amount of risk organization willing to accept
  - Strategic decision
  - Board-level
  - Guides risk decisions

- [ ] **Risk tolerance**
  - Acceptable variation from risk appetite
  - Specific thresholds
  - Operational level
  - Technical limits

- [ ] **Risk threshold**
  - Limit of acceptable risk
  - Trigger for action
  - Risk vs. cost balance

#### Video 2: Risk Analysis
- [ ] **Watched Professor Messer's "Risk Analysis" video**

##### Risk Analysis Methods
- [ ] **Asset identification**
  - Inventory assets
  - Determine value
  - Classify by importance
  - Prioritize protection

- [ ] **Threat identification**
  - Internal threats
  - External threats
  - Natural disasters
  - Human threats
  - Technical threats

- [ ] **Vulnerability identification**
  - Scanning
  - Assessments
  - Pentesting
  - Security audits

##### Quantitative Analysis
- [ ] **Asset Value (AV)**
  - Monetary value of asset
  - Replacement cost
  - Revenue generation
  - Strategic value

- [ ] **Exposure Factor (EF)**
  - Percentage of asset loss
  - If threat realized
  - 0% to 100%

- [ ] **Single Loss Expectancy (SLE)**
  - SLE = AV × EF
  - Loss from single incident
  - Monetary value

- [ ] **Annual Rate of Occurrence (ARO)**
  - Expected frequency per year
  - Historical data
  - Probability estimate

- [ ] **Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE)**
  - ALE = SLE × ARO
  - Expected annual loss
  - Cost-benefit analysis
  - Justifies controls

##### Risk Register
- [ ] **Risk register components**
  - Risk description
  - Risk owner
  - Likelihood and impact
  - Current controls
  - Risk score/rating
  - Mitigation plans
  - Status tracking

- [ ] **Risk register maintenance**
  - Regular updates
  - Continuous monitoring
  - New risk identification
  - Control effectiveness

#### Video 3: Business Impact Analysis
- [ ] **Watched Professor Messer's "Business Impact Analysis" video**

##### BIA Process
- [ ] **Business Impact Analysis (BIA)**
  - Identify critical functions
  - Determine impact of disruption
  - Prioritize recovery
  - Resource requirements
  - Dependencies

- [ ] **Impact categories**
  - Financial impact
  - Reputational impact
  - Operational impact
  - Legal/regulatory impact
  - Customer impact

##### Recovery Metrics
- [ ] **Maximum Tolerable Downtime (MTD)**
  - Longest acceptable outage
  - Before severe consequences
  - Business-driven
  - Per critical function

- [ ] **Recovery Time Objective (RTO)**
  - Target recovery time
  - Return to operation
  - Must be < MTD
  - Technical goal

- [ ] **Recovery Point Objective (RPO)**
  - Acceptable data loss
  - Point-in-time recovery
  - Backup frequency
  - Data criticality

- [ ] **Mean Time to Repair (MTTR)**
  - Average repair time
  - Historical metric
  - Incident response efficiency
  - Continuous improvement

- [ ] **Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF)**
  - Average time between incidents
  - Reliability metric
  - Hardware specifications
  - Predictive maintenance

##### Critical Functions
- [ ] **Mission-essential functions**
  - Core business operations
  - Revenue generation
  - Customer service
  - Critical dependencies

- [ ] **Single Point of Failure (SPOF)**
  - Component whose failure stops system
  - Eliminate where possible
  - Redundancy planning
  - High-risk areas

#### Video 4: Risk Management Strategies
- [ ] **Watched Professor Messer's "Risk Management Strategies" video**

##### Risk Response Strategies
- [ ] **Risk acceptance**
  - Accept the risk
  - No additional controls
  - Cost exceeds benefit
  - Documented decision
  - Management approval required

- [ ] **Risk avoidance**
  - Eliminate the risk
  - Stop the activity
  - Change plans
  - Different approach

- [ ] **Risk transference (Risk sharing)**
  - Transfer risk to third party
  - Insurance
  - Contracts and SLAs
  - Outsourcing
  - Cloud services

- [ ] **Risk mitigation (Risk reduction)**
  - Reduce likelihood or impact
  - Implement controls
  - Most common strategy
  - Security measures

##### Risk Monitoring
- [ ] **Continuous monitoring**
  - Ongoing assessment
  - Risk changes over time
  - New threats emerge
  - Control effectiveness

- [ ] **Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)**
  - Early warning signs
  - Proactive monitoring
  - Trending data
  - Predictive metrics

- [ ] **Risk reporting**
  - Executive dashboards
  - Risk heat maps
  - Trend analysis
  - Compliance status

## Key Terms & Definitions

**Term** | **Definition**
---------|---------------
**Governance** | Framework of policies, processes, and controls
**Risk** | Likelihood of threat exploiting vulnerability with negative impact
**Risk Appetite** | Amount of risk organization is willing to accept
**Risk Tolerance** | Acceptable variation from risk appetite
**ALE** | Annualized Loss Expectancy - expected annual loss from risk
**SLE** | Single Loss Expectancy - loss from single incident
**ARO** | Annual Rate of Occurrence - expected frequency per year
**RTO** | Recovery Time Objective - target recovery time
**RPO** | Recovery Point Objective - acceptable data loss
**MTD** | Maximum Tolerable Downtime - longest acceptable outage
**MTTR** | Mean Time to Repair - average repair time
